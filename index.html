Andy Hoang

I'm a multidisciplinary product designer and creative generalist.

Each colored box is an important milestone in my professional journey.
I blend product strategy, interaction, visual design and motion prototyping to deliver research-validated solutions.
For every portfolio there is an untold story. I used to be terrible at drawing, I majored in natural science in high school, and I never thought I could do anything creative.
In fact, I wasn't planning to study design. I used to want to be an army officer and I took the entrance exam to the Military Technical Academy in 2015. And you know, I failed =)))
I studied graphic design according to my mother's wish, but I only really got serious when in my sophomore year, I got a freelance project in UI/UX Design, and I thought"Oh, I'll do it something more useful than just graphic design".
From there on out, I spent every hour outside of the classroom learning about product design, user research and front end development. I was obsessed. That obsession has been fueling both my professional and personal pursuits, and it's turned me into the generalist I am today.
Below are a few highlights of my design career thus far. Ispecialize in product design, but I can take on a number of other roles related to the field of graphic design, website building... Enjoy.
