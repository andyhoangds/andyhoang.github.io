Project Summary
My product is built with a "user-centered" philosophy, featuring intelligent and modern functionalities. You will easily find everything you need with just a few simple actions, thanks to the intuitive interface and optimized design for all devices. From browsing, streamlining processes, selecting drivers, and organizing task groups, to managing personal tasks, the product from Son Thanh Holdings will be a powerful assistant in saving time and enhancing efficiency in transportation and logistics coordination at border crossings.
Essentially, it is designed to facilitate the process of goods transportation, vehicle coordination, and pairing of transport trucks. It is divided into three systems: Customer, Admin, and Driver.

Customers will create a transportation order, fill in information about the type of goods, weight, mode of transport, etc., and send it to the Admin for vehicle coordination:
Afterward, the Admin team at Son Thanh will receive the request, organize the task groups, coordinate the vehicles, or may reject the order if it does not meet the transportation requirements:
After the Admin has accepted the service, the orders will be displayed on the dedicated Driver application. The drivers will then carry out the transportation, entering the goods information to check and compare the actual quantity of goods with the quantity specified by the Customer in the order to calculate the appropriate costs.
The Result
The product is highly praised by internal operations staff, customers, and drivers for its ease of use and user-friendly interface. The clear information helps streamline operations, making them more efficient and cost-effective, whereas previously, the processes were entirely manual.
