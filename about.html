About Andy

I’m a product designer living in North Tu Liem, Hanoi. I design beautiful, functional interfaces that better people’s lives. I’ve spent the last 5 years across different surfaces of design: Photographer of wedding photography studio, Graphic design, Print on Demand and UI/UX Design. Now, I focus on user research, native mobile design, UX and visual design, applying these approaches to problems of all different shapes and sizes.

I’m also a creative generalist. Outside of work, you’ll find me telling stories through various creative mediums, whether it’s through painting or drawing, playing the guitar, photography, Radio personal channel's MC, or even volunteering.
